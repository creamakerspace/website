Class Offerrings

Art and Robots

Whether the thought of building a robot sounds intimidating or exciting, this class is for you. This is a light-hearted, artistic introduction to robots, digital circuits, and code suitable for ages 8 to 108. You'll design a simple craft robot, and we'll walk through the process of incorporating a micro:bit to bring it to life.

This class does not require any prior knowledge about coding or digital electronics.

Sign Up

Laser Engraving: Edge Lit Acrylic

Learn how to safely operate the laser for both cutting and engraving. You'll get a hands on opportunity to cut and engrave an edge-lit acrylic sign. You'll also get a basic introduction to using Lightburn for laser work. Class includes materials, and you will get to take home your laser engraved project.

Completion of this class provides certification to independently use the laser cutter in your future projects.

Sign Up

Woodshop 101: Cutting & Shaping

This class will provide you with an introduction to many of the tools in the woodshop. We'll compare and contrast the various saws in the shop, and we'll discuss sanding, routing, and planing. And of course, we'll do this all hands-on in the shop.

This class does not require any prior experience with woodworking.

This class is required to use most of the woodshop equipment.

Sign Up

CNC Router: Sign Making

Unleash your creativity and learn the art of sign making! This workshop will provide a comprehensive introduction to the Shapeoko 5 CNC router. We'll guide you through the end-to-end process of designing a custom sign, and teach you how to safely operate the CNC router. At the end of this class, you'll take home your own custom sign!

Completion of this class provides certification to independently use the CNC Router in your future projects.

Sign Up

Intro to 3D Printing

Immerse yourself in the world of 3D printing! In this hands-on class, we'll guide you through the 3D printing process. You'll learn how to find a vast number of freely available 3D models, gain an understanding of different kinds of filaments, and learn how to operate the 3D printer. We'll dive into the process of slicing 3D models together, and explore the most common 3D printing issues to ensure you're 3D printing journey is productive.

Completion of this class provides certification to independently use the 3D printer your future projects.
